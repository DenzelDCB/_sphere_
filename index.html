Who We Are

Our Mission

Founded in 2015, Apex Scholars has been at the forefront of student development. We believe that education extends beyond the classroom walls. Our organization provides students with the tools they need to succeed in the real world.


Through our mentorship programs and peer-to-peer tutoring, we have helped over 500 students achieve their academic goals while fostering a sense of community and belonging.

Organization Moments

Common Questions

How can I join the organization? +

Simply fill out the membership form during the first two weeks of each semester. We welcome all majors!

Are there any membership fees? +

To support our events and provide snacks, there is a small $15 fee per semester.

What is the time commitment? +

We recommend at least 2 hours a week for meetings and one weekend event per month.
